Solar Air Conditioner


What is a Solar Air Conditioner?

It works like a conventional air conditioner. The 2 major differences are: Existing A/C uses only electricity grid and takes high wattage to run whereas solar AC takes very low wattage. Solar AC the MGM Technology (Compressor) works like an Accelerator in a car. When Compressor needs more power, it gives more power and vice versa. The speed and power of the compressor is adjusted appropriately.

What are the benefits of Solar Air Conditioner?

This Air Conditioner will run continuously but will draw only that much power that is required to keep the temperature stable at the level desired. So it automatically adjusts its capacity based on the requirement of the room it is cooling. Thus drawing much less power and consuming lesser units of electricity.
